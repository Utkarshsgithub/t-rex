Metacenter

You will be able to shop without leaving your home!

What is the Metacenter?

The metacenter is a shopping center in the T-Rex metaverse!

Why will this revolutionize the world of online shopping?

Because it's a new way that is both pleasant and efficient to shop on the internet! Moreover at T-Rex we will be particularly sharp on the security of the user of the robots will be put in place to avoid fraud or aggression.

What are the advantages of this metacenter?

  • More pleasant than being on a 2d site
  • You will be able to interact with products and sellers
  • Interesting and entertaining than a lambda site

How to support the project?

To support the project you can buy land on our Metacenter or just join our discord and follow us on social networks!

Does T-Rex have other projects?

Yes, the T-Rex metaverse won't just stop at the mall; recreation buildings as well as housing will be integrated over time!
